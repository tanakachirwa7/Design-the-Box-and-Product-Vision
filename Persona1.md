# Persona: Alex Johnson, a Data Analyst

## Background
Alex is a junior data analyst working at a mid-sized retail company. He has a strong foundation in statistics and SQL but limited experience building full data pipelines from scratch. He frequently works with messy datasets coming from multiple sources such as CSV files, APIs, and internal databases.

## Goals
- Quickly clean and transform raw data into usable formats
- Generate accurate reports for business stakeholders
- Reduce the amount of manual data cleaning work

## Needs
- An automated, repeatable data cleaning process
- Clear error messages when data issues occur
- Easy-to-read summary reports and visual outputs

## Frustrations
- Spending hours manually fixing inconsistent data
- Pipelines breaking without clear explanations
- Reports that are difficult to reproduce or update

## Technical Comfort
Moderate to high. Comfortable with Python, SQL, and basic command-line tools.
