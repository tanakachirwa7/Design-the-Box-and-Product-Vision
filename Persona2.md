# Persona: Maria Lopez, a Business Manager

## Background
Maria is a business operations manager who oversees sales performance and inventory planning. She does not write code but relies heavily on reports generated by the data team to make decisions.

## Goals
- Understand trends in sales and inventory quickly
- Make data-driven decisions without needing technical expertise
- Receive reports on a consistent schedule

## Needs
- Clear, well-formatted reports with summaries
- Visualizations that highlight key insights
- Confidence that the data is accurate and up-to-date

## Frustrations
- Reports that are delayed or inconsistent
- Technical explanations that are hard to understand
- Needing to request repeated changes to reports

## Technical Comfort
Low. Prefers dashboards, PDFs, or spreadsheets over raw data files.
